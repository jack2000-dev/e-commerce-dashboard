# Scoring Rubric

Use this rubric with `score.md`.

## Correctness: 20 points

- 0-5: Outputs are missing or mostly wrong.
- 6-10: Basic workflow exists but has major logic gaps.
- 11-15: Mostly correct with small issues.
- 16-20: Correct, coherent, and handles expected cases.

## Reproducibility: 15 points

- 0-5: Hard to run.
- 6-10: Runs with manual fixes.
- 11-13: Clear setup and run steps.
- 14-15: Fully reproducible with clean environment instructions.

## SQL And Data Logic: 15 points

- 0-5: Unclear joins, grain, or aggregations.
- 6-10: Basic SQL works but has modeling risks.
- 11-13: Correct logic with understandable structure.
- 14-15: Strong SQL, clear grain, and defensible metrics.

## Python Or Pipeline Quality: 15 points

- 0-5: Script is fragile or incomplete.
- 6-10: Works for happy path.
- 11-13: Organized and handles common issues.
- 14-15: Clean, testable, and maintainable.

## Testing And Validation: 15 points

- 0-5: Little or no validation.
- 6-10: Some checks but incomplete.
- 11-13: Good coverage of important risks.
- 14-15: Strong validation with clear failure meaning.

## Documentation: 10 points

- 0-3: Missing or confusing.
- 4-7: Usable but incomplete.
- 8-10: Clear setup, architecture, and outputs.

## Interview Readiness: 10 points

- 0-3: Cannot explain the project clearly.
- 4-7: Explains basics but misses tradeoffs or tests.
- 8-10: Clear, confident, and specific explanation.
